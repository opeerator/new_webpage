Ali Yamini

MASc, ECE, University of Waterloo, 2025.

Ali is a research associate at the Social and Intelligent Robotics Research Lab, with over 7 years of experience in software development. He led the creation of Mirrly, a humanoid robot for CRI, co-founded two software startups, and has worked on swarm navigation using reinforcement learning, image-processing systems, and full-stack applications across web and mobile platforms.
